# UFSL Bracket — Beta Tester Guide

Hey! Thanks for testing UFSL Bracket before launch. Here's what you need to know:

## What you're testing
This is a REAL bracket pool app launching for Selection Sunday (March 16). Your feedback directly shapes what ships.

## How to join
1. Open: https://ufsl-bracket-test.vercel.app/join/BETA2025
2. Sign in with Google (takes ~30 seconds)
3. Submit your bracket picks

## The simulator
We're running a **simulated tournament** so you can experience the full app — live scores, leaderboard updates, upset alerts — without waiting for real games.

Shawn (the commissioner) will advance rounds periodically. After each round:
- Your score updates automatically
- You'll see who got knocked out
- Smack talk is highly encouraged

## What we want you to try
- [ ] Join the pool and submit a bracket
- [ ] Check the leaderboard after a round advances
- [ ] Post something in Smack Talk
- [ ] View someone else's bracket (tap their name on the leaderboard)
- [ ] Try on mobile AND desktop if you can

## What to report
Text Shawn anything that feels broken, confusing, or just bad. No bug too small. Especially:
- Anything that didn't work on your phone
- Anything confusing about the bracket picker
- Any page that loaded slowly or crashed

Thanks for being part of this! 🏀
